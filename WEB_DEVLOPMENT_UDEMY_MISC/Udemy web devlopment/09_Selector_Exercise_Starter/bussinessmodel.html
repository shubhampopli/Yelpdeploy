THE BUSINESS MODEL

    The first year plan would be to put all the high-demand jobs in the menu option like:-
  1. Developer Jobs
  2. Marketing Jobs
  3. Content Writer jobs
  4. Graphic Designer
  5. This will create a good reputation and spread the word about our platform.
    The second year plan would be to go offline to grab the huge unskilled labor with easy jobs like-
  • Standing in a Queue
  • Walking a Dog
  • Baby Sitting
  • Car Washing, etc

The third year plan would be to add jobs which have low demands depending on the market forces but high supply. This will provide opportunity to all for earning a living. In the coming years, we will be providing option for the industries for bulk hiring(Group of people who can do the work efficiently), where they can work over a project and submit a full fledged project at the end.

  1. We would generate Profit by our Payment method and commission
  2. When the Client pays for the job, the Money will be transferred to a “Escrow Account” rather than to the Freelancer’s account.
  3. Freelancer would get Notified about this and would be incentivized to work judiciously
  4. After the work is delivered, the Freelancer would get the fee from which we will take our commission
  5. The Money in the “Escrow Account ” would earn us security and Revenue
